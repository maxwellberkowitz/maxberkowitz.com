Hi there! My name is Max, and this is my website. Welcome! On this site, you will find a variety of content, but it will all fall under one common theme: the journey of young creators, thinkers, and innovators on the path to achieving what matters most to us – whatever that may be. In creating this website, I hope to share my thoughts on the world from the perspective of a student and young professional. I hope to spark conversation around what motivates you and I to pursue our greatest ambitions. Most importantly (and maybe optimistically), I hope I might inspire you to wake up tomorrow and put your nose to the grindstone on whatever matters most to you – because I believe life is best when you’re spending it doing something you love. So please, take a look around, and hit up the Contact section let me know what you think! There’s nothing I welcome more than a good conversation. Talk soon, Max

About The Blog

This is a student lifestyle blog with an emphasis on the path to the professional world and managing all that life throws us along the way. I intend to cast the net wide; whether you’re interested in the fine art of job hunting, the journey towards academic success, or simply staying sane and happy in the millennial rate race, I believe there is something here for you. Take a read and find out!

Note About The Website

This website is the toddler-evolved version of a thought-baby which was first conceived around December of 2017. Since then, I have poured every available moment into its growth. However, thought-baby parenting is a learning process, and I would happily accept all the help I can get! This is my first time blogging, website designing, and writing web-related code (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, etc.), and I am not using WordPress or any equivalent design tools. So, if you are going through this website thinking: "Hm, I definitely think that this part could be done better” then let me know!!! I don't just "welcome" your criticism – I really want it.
