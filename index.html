Max Berkowitz

Engineer, Creator, Designer and Writer

Hi there! My name is Max, and this is my website. Welcome! On this site, you will find a variety of content, but it will all fall under one common theme: the journey of young creators, thinkers, and innovators on the path to achieving what matters most to us – whatever that may be. In creating this website, I hope to share my thoughts on the world from the perspective of a student and young professional. I hope to spark conversation around what motivates you and I to pursue our greatest ambitions. Most importantly (and maybe optimistically), I hope I might inspire you to wake up tomorrow and put your nose to the grindstone on whatever matters most to you – because I believe life is best when you’re spending it doing something you love. So please, take a look around. And hit up the Contact section let me know what you think! There’s nothing I welcome more than a good conversation. Talk soon, Max
